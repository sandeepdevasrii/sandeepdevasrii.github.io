Reduce Effective Area of Touchpad in linux

First, check what are the values of maximum horizontal and vertical dimensions, i.e., (width and height) of the touchpad, using the following command.

less /var/log/Xorg.0.log | grep -i range
This will produce an ouput similar to the following: 
[ 20.616] (--) synaptics: SynPS/2 Synaptics TouchPad: x-axis range 1472 - 5638 (res 37)
[ 20.616] (--) synaptics: SynPS/2 Synaptics TouchPad: y-axis range 1408 - 4714 (res 59)
Now, the width of the touchpad is 5638, and the height is 4714 The width and height can now be adjusted as, 
synclient AreaRightEdge=5000 AreaLeftEdge=2000 AreaBottomEdge=4000
where, AreaLeftEdge is the point on the horizontal axis, from where, the area of the touchpad begins, AreaRightEdge corresponds to the point where the touchpad area ends, and so on.
